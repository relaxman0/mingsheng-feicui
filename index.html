Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how [live-msg] ("we", "our") handles data when you interact with our Facebook Page Live broadcasts. We are committed to protecting user privacy and complying with Meta's Platform Policies.

1. Data Collection

Our application accesses the following information via Meta Graph API during Live sessions:

  • Public Profile: Name and Profile ID (App-Scoped ID).
  • Comment Content: The text content you post in the live chat.
  • Timestamps: When your comment was created.

2. Purpose of Data Use

We use this data exclusively for real-time interaction, including:

  • Displaying your name on the live stream when responding to questions.
  • Identifying participants for interactive lucky draws.
  • Ensuring community safety through comment moderation.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store your personal data permanently. Most data is processed in real-time and cleared after the broadcast ends.

User Data Deletion: You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. To do so, please contact us via our Facebook Page or email: [zhyb.box@gmail.com]. We will process your request within 24 hours.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.
